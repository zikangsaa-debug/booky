Source Origin: Our Eco Story

The materials used in the Booky Eco Bookmark Kit come from post-consumer recycled paper such as used notebooks, worksheets, office paper waste, and recycled cardboard packaging.

These materials are collected, cleaned, and processed into new paper sheets, giving them a second life. Decorative elements are made from recycled kraft paper and natural fibres like jute or cotton.

Our glue and ink are water-based and non-toxic, making the kit safe for students and environmentally friendly.
